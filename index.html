What happens if you fall into the BH?

As you get closer and closer to the black hole event horizon, the observer from far away sees your movements slowing down due to the time dilation, which takes place around the strong gravitation pull. At the moment you fall into the black hole, the observer sees you are still forever though you already fall into the black hole. You yourself will see everything normal until you fall inside, you will see the history of the universe quickly passing through your view...
